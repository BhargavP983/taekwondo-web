About us

Taekwondo Association of Andhra Pradesh (APTA) is the State Unit, affiliated to the Taekwondo Federation of India and Andhra Pradesh State Sports Council.

"KUKKIWON is the World Headquarter of Taekwondo which was founded in 1972. On 28th May 1973 The World Taekwondo Federation (WTF) came into existence. In 1974 Taekwondo was admitted to Asian Games as an official event. In 1975 the sport of Taekwondo was admitted by the General Association of International Sports Federation (GAISF). The WTF became an International Olympic Committee recognized sports Federation in 1980 Making Taekwondo an Olympics sport."
